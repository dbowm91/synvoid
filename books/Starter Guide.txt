Welcome to Terminal Book Reader!

To get started, you can place EPUB or TXT files in the 'books' directory.
Alternatively, press Tab to switch to the Downloader pane and search Project Gutenberg.

Shortcuts:
- Tab: Switch between panes (Library/Downloader and Reader)
- j/k or Down/Up: Navigate lists and scroll text
- Enter: Open a book or download it
- /: Search library or online store
- : (colon): Command mode
- Ctrl+h: View all shortcuts
- q: Quit application
